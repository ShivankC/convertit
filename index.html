  • The Idea

    There comes a time in every students life when they struggle. Struggle to cope with the endless barrage of nonsensical math homework that they are given every single day. The idea for this website came to me on one such day.
    A website created for the students, by a student.

  • The Mission

    At the time the inspiration for this project struck me, I was learning JavaScript, and I immediately knew it was meant to be. Since this website was for students, it had to be something easy and quick to use in a pickle but also something informative someone could use to cram before an exam, and thus, convertIT was born.
